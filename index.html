About Theoremica

Welcome to Theoremica, an innovative visual proof builder designed to make learning and practicing mathematical proofs more accessible, interactive, and engaging. Whether you're a student just beginning your journey into formal mathematics or an experienced mathematician looking to explore Lean 4 theorem proving, Theoremica provides the tools you need.

Theoremica combines the power of visual block-based programming with formal mathematical notation to help you construct rigorous proofs. Instead of struggling with syntax or formal proof systems, you can focus on the logic and reasoning behind each step. Our intuitive drag-and-drop interface makes it easy to build complex proofs by connecting logical blocks together.

Discrete Mathematics - MATH 240

Explore key theorems and proofs from McGill University's MATH 240 (Discrete Mathematics) course. This comprehensive collection covers fundamental concepts in discrete mathematics including:

  • Proof Techniques - Mathematical induction, pigeonhole principle, and proof by contradiction
  • Set Theory - Set operations, relations, and equivalence classes
  • Logic - Propositional logic, Boolean algebra, and logical equivalences
  • Combinatorics - Permutations, combinations, and counting principles
  • Graph Theory - Basic graph concepts, paths, and the handshaking lemma
  • Number Theory - Divisibility, modular arithmetic, and the Euclidean algorithm

With 12 carefully selected theorems and proofs, this course provides a solid foundation in discrete mathematics. Each proof is accompanied by clear statements and interactive tools to help you understand and construct formal proofs.
